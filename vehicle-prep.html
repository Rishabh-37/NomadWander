Vehicle Preparation Guide

Complete preparation checklists for cars, motorbikes, and electric vehicles

Pre-Trip Checklist

  • Check tire pressure and tread
  • Engine oil level and quality
  • Brake fluid and brake pads
  • Coolant level
  • Battery terminals and charge
  • Lights and indicators
  • Windshield wipers
  • Air filter condition
  • Fuel system check
  • Steering and suspension

Emergency Kit

  • Spare tire and jack
  • Jumper cables
  • Basic tool kit
  • Emergency contact numbers
  • First aid kit
  • Flashlight and batteries
  • Emergency water and snacks
  • Warning triangles
  • Fire extinguisher
  • Multi-tool or Swiss knife

What to Do During a Breakdown

1

Safety First

Move to a safe location, turn on hazard lights, and set up warning triangles

2

Assess the Problem

Check for obvious issues: flat tire, overheating, strange noises

3

Call for Help

Contact roadside assistance, local mechanics, or emergency services

4

Stay Safe

Wait in a safe location away from traffic until help arrives
