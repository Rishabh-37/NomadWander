EV Charging Tips

Plan Your Route

Always plan charging stops before starting your journey. Keep backup options in case your primary station is occupied.

Use Charging Apps

Download multiple charging network apps to check real-time availability and book slots in advance.

Weather Considerations

Cold weather can reduce battery range by 20-40%. Plan for additional charging stops during winter trips.

Optimal Charging

Charge between 20-80% for fastest speeds. Avoid charging to 100% unless necessary for long distances.

Payment Methods

Keep multiple payment options ready - apps, RFID cards, and contactless payments for different networks.

Carry Adapters

Bring necessary charging adapters and cables. Some stations may not have the connector type for your vehicle.
