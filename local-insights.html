Hidden Waterfall near Bhimtal

There's a secret waterfall just 2km off the main road to Bhimtal. Take the dirt path near the old temple - it's worth the 20-minute hike!

Best Street Food in Old Delhi

Skip the touristy spots and head to Gali Paranthe Wali early morning. Try the aloo paratha at Pt. Gaya Prasad's shop - it's been serving since 1872!
